\subsection{Price Aggregation}

Total order flow in the stablecoin market is given by:
\begin{equation}
O_s = \int_0^{n_s} o_j \, dj = \kappa \int_0^{n_s} (\theta + \nu_j) \, dj.
\end{equation}

Normalizing by participation:
\begin{equation}
\bar{O}_s = \frac{O_s}{\kappa n_s} = \theta + \frac{1}{n_s} \int_0^{n_s} \nu_j \, dj.
\end{equation}

By the law of large numbers, the noise term has variance:
\begin{equation}
\mathrm{Var}\left(\frac{1}{n_s} \int_0^{n_s} \nu_j \, dj\right) = \frac{\sigma_\nu^2}{n_s}.
\end{equation}

Thus, the stablecoin price reveals a public signal:
\begin{equation}
s = \theta + \eta, \quad \eta \sim \mathcal{N}\left(0, \frac{\sigma_\nu^2}{n_s}\right).
\end{equation}
