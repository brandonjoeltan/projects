\section{Model}

\subsection{Environment}

The economy is characterized by an overvaluation parameter theta. Higher values of theta correspond to a more overvalued official exchange rate.

\subsection{FX Demand}

Households demand foreign currency for two reasons:

Q_FX = Q_hedge + Q_run

Hedging demand is given by:

Q_hedge = max(0, chi_h * theta - eta_h * p_FX)

Run demand depends on crisis beliefs and coordination:

Q_run = gamma_r * (1 + psi * omega_s) * Lambda(pi)

\subsection{Crisis}

A crisis occurs when aggregate FX demand exceeds available reserves:

pi = Lambda(Q_FX - (alpha - delta * theta))

\subsection{Stablecoins}

Stablecoins reduce transaction costs and improve the precision of public signals, increasing coordination among agents.
